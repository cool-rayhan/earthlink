What is EARTHLINK

Our Earth is a complex system of interconnected environmental processes, where changes in one aspect of the environment can lead to cascading effects throughout the planet. Our web app, EarthLink, aims to raise awareness of this interconnectedness by offering users a real-time, interactive visualization of Earth's systems and how human activities—particularly climate change—disrupt this delicate balance. EarthLink will enable users simulate climate change scenarios, and visualize how shifts in one system, such as rising CO₂ levels or deforestation, can have wide-ranging impacts on other systems, such as air quality, weather patterns, and biodiversity. Our app will provide both a global view and localized insights to help users understand the interconnected effects of environmental change.

EarthLink,
is the dedicated website for the "Imagine Connected Earth" project. It features interactive data visualizations and an engaging EarthLink Counter that dynamically tracks and displays key metrics and milestones. Designed with a user-friendly interface, EarthLink provides a comprehensive platform to explore and understand the interconnected systems of our planet, offering educational resources and real-time insights into environmental data.
